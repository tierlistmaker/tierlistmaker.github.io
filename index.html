What is a tier list?

A tier list is a ranking system that organizes items into ordered categories — most commonly labeled S, A, B, C, and D — from best to worst. Originating in competitive gaming communities, tier lists have become a universal way for fans and enthusiasts to compare, debate, and share opinions about virtually any topic.

A free online tier list maker like ours lets anyone create a tier list directly in their browser without downloading software or signing up for an account. You can rank characters from your favorite game, movies in a franchise, foods, sports teams, or anything else that matters to you.

Once you've finished ranking, you can download your tier list as an image or share a link. It's one of the most popular formats for community discussions on Reddit, Discord, and social media — because a tier list makes your opinions instantly visual and easy to debate.
